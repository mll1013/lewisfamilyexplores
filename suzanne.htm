The Lord of the Rings

J. R. R. Tolkien's trilogy "The Lord of the Rings" has practically a cult following.  Tolkien invented this fantasy world along with several "languages" to go along with it.  The book is an incredible tale of good versus evil.  If you have never read them, they are well worth the effort.  If you have read them, it couldn't hurt to revisit.
