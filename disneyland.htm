Disneyland
After leaving Southern California for Idaho, we decided to go back so we could make our first family trip to Disneyland.  We wanted to wait until Raymond was "regulation" height for most of the rides, and naturally that happened after we moved over 1000 miles away from Anaheim. In order to get there, we opted to fly on Jet Blue out of Salt Lake City, since it was much cheaper than our other airline options.  However, this also gave us a chance to visit Temple Square in Salt Lake as well as the Malad Gorge in Idaho.
