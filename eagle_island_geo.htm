Eagle Island Geocaching


When we found out that there were 17 geocaches at Eagle Island State Park, located between two forks of the Boise River, we were excited to explore and find.  We were a perfect 5-for-5 on the day for caches we hunted, but there are still a dozen left to find... guess we'll just have to head out there another day!
