Granite Falls Hike
In the Pacific Northwest, the LDS Church owns and operates several Church Parks.  Our stake is assigned to Cascade Park in Arlington, WA.  We service the park twice a year clearing brush, cutting wood, and performing other clean-up tasks.  After going to help during the Autumn clean-up, we decided to head over to Granite Falls, which is nearby to the park.  It was a short stroll between towering granite cliffs and a lush forest to find the beautiful cascade.
