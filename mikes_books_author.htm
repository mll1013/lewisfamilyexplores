Mike's Books Page

I love to read.  There's just one problem, however.  I never find or make the time to do it.  But, I do have some solutions.  Sometimes, I check out books on tape from one of the local public libraries and listen during my commute to and from work.  Other times, I'll download audiobooks from LivriVox, and listen while doing work around the house.  On business trips, I take the opportunity to read on the airplane. 

So, by making some time here and there, I've been able to read the following list of books... mostly over the last 10 years or so.  (In the table, 10 is the highest rating, 1 is the lowest.)
