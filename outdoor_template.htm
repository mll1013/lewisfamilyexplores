Chocolate Gulch Hike
Shortly after moving to Idaho, we wanted to experience some of the Fall color in our area.  Hearing about the Big Wood River area near Sun Valley Ski Resort, we opted to take a weekend drive and check it out.  While we went a little late in the season, we were still very impressed with the yellows, reds, and oranges mixed in with the evergreens of the elevation that still abounded in the area.
