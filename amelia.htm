The Hunger Games

Amelia still shows an immense talent in reading and writing.  As a result, she has started an honors English track in middle school.  While she mostly enjoys fantasy, one her favorite sets of books right now is the The Hunger Games .  The best-selling dystopic sci-fi thriller has been a tremendous success for youth author, Suzanne Collins.  In fact, the series has been so popular, that it has even toppled Harry Potter out of the number one spot on the New York Times best-seller list for children's book series.  Amelia loved the first movie and can't wait for the next.
