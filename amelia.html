Fairy Realm

For a couple of years now, Amelia has been reading series-based chapter books, and she will be quick to point out that she's read from A-to-Z... literally!  The A-Z Mysteries was the first series books she had read.  Since then, she's read several other series, and particularly enjoys fantasy books.  The series Fairy Realm by Emily Rodda is her absolute favorite so far.  Amelia loves to read and has read well over 100 books in the last three years or so!
